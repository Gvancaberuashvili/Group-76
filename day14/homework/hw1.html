HOUSES

  • Gryffindor - values bravery, courage, and chivalry.

    • Founded by Godric Gryffindor, this house values bravery, courage, and daring.
    Its students are often bold and determined, willing to stand up for what they believe in.
    The house colors are red and gold, and its symbol is a lion.
    Famous members include Harry Potter, Hermione Granger, and Ron Weasley.

  • Hufflepuff - values loyalty, kindness, and hard work.

    • Founded by Helga Hufflepuff, this house values loyalty, patience, and fairness.
    Its students are known for being kind, hardworking, and inclusive of everyone.
    The house colors are yellow and black, and its symbol is a badger.
    Famous members include Cedric Diggory and Newt Scamander.

  • Ravenclaw - values intelligence, creativity, and wisdom.

    • Founded by Rowena Ravenclaw, this house values intelligence, wit, and creativity.
    Its students are curious thinkers who love learning and problem-solving.
    the house colors are blue and bronze (silver in the movies), and its symbol is an eagle.
    Famous members include Luna Lovegood and Cho Chang.

  • Slytherin - values ambition, cunning, and resourcefulness.

    • Founded by Salazar Slytherin, this house values ambition, cunning, and resourcefulness.
    Its students are often determined to achieve greatness, sometimes by bending the rules.
    The house colors are green and silver, and its symbol is a serpent.
    Famous members include Severus Snape, Draco Malfoy, and Lord Voldemort.
