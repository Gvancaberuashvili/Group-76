Game of Thrones is a fantasy series set in the fictional land of Westeros,
where noble families compete for the Iron Throne and control over the Seven Kingdoms.
Political plots, betrayals, and shifting alliances drive the struggle for power,
while an ancient supernatural threat — the White Walkers — begins to emerge from the far North.
Alongside the human conflicts, dragons, magic, and prophecies return to the world, shaping the fates of key characters.
The story weaves together themes of loyalty, ambition, love, and survival in a brutal and unpredictable world.

the main characters

🦁 Tyrion Lannister – The witty, intelligent youngest son of House Lannister, often underestimated because of his height but skilled in politics and strategy.
🐺 Jon Snow – The illegitimate son of House Stark, raised in the North. He joins the Night’s Watch, faces threats beyond the Wall, and struggles with questions about his identity.
🐉 Daenerys Targaryen – The last surviving member of a deposed royal family, determined to reclaim the Iron Throne. She rises from exile to command armies and dragons.
❄️ Arya Stark – A fierce and independent Stark daughter who trains as an assassin, seeking justice for her family.
🦌 Stannis Baratheon – A rigid and duty-driven claimant to the throne who believes he is the rightful king.
🦁 Cersei Lannister – Queen of the Seven Kingdoms for much of the story,
fiercely protective of her children and willing to do anything to keep power.
🐺 Sansa Stark – Initially a naïve noble girl, she matures into a politically savvy leader after surviving betrayal and manipulation.
